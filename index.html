About the Forum
Bridging AI Innovation with Life Sciences

As artificial intelligence and biotechnology reshape the future of agriculture, food science, drug discovery, and healthcare, this forum brings together leading experts, scholars, and industry pioneers to explore how cross-disciplinary innovation can address humanity's most pressing challenges.

The forum features two keynote speeches, four panel discussions covering the key domains of precision agriculture, food technology, drug discovery, and smart healthcare, followed by a roundtable session on the ethical and regulatory challenges of AI and biotechnology.

A key highlight is the live demonstration of a privacy-preserving federated learning toolkit developed at XJTLU, enabling cross-institutional collaboration without sharing raw data — with proven results across medical imaging (99.17% R²), embodied intelligence (+3.4% aggregation boost), and 87% communication cost reduction.
